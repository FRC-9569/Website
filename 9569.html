9569 SILVER KNIGHTS

ABOUT US

9569 Silver Knights Robotics is a team of dedicated high school students based in Markham, Ontario who participate in the FIRST Robotics Competition every year, fostering STEM learning within our community. Our mission is to provide a safe and inclusive environment where students can explore their passions and gain hands-on experience in the various fields involved in FRC including but not limited to: engineering, computer aided design, programming, art, and business.
